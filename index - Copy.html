Hi, my name is Khanh N. Dang and I joined ASLab the Uni. of Aizu as a doctor student in October 2014. My research is about Reliable Vision Processing with Multi-cores and Network-on-Chip.

I am currently pursueing a comprehensive soft errors and hard faults tolerance work-flow for Network-on-Chip systems.
I am also developing an analytical model for reliability assessment which can help designers improve their works' quality. The very first idea will be published on Asian Test Symposium '16.

Before The Uni. of Aizu, I worked as researcher in SISLAB at the University of Engineering and Technology, VNUH (VNUH-UET). I have an M.S. from Uni. of Paris XI, and B.S. degrees in electronics and telecomunication from the VNUH-UET.

I wrote the inter-prediction block of VENGME Project, which is the first H.264/AVC full-hardware encoder in Vietnam.
