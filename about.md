**Hi, my name is Khanh!**<br><br>
_Present:_

I am a Ph.D. student at [Adaptive Systems Laboratory](http://adaptive.u-aizu.ac.jp) of the [University of Aizu](http://www.u-aizu.ac.jp/en/).  <br>
